The Amalfi Coast lives up to its reputation — and then quietly exceeds it. Ten days across Amalfi, Ravello, and Positano with a day trip to Capri. This guide covers only what we'd do again, with every recommendation sourced so you know exactly why it made the list.

One thing I wish I'd known: base yourself in Ravello. Quieter, higher up, easier for everything. Positano is best as a day trip in September — beautiful but heaving with tourists.

01
Ristorante Da Gemma
📍 Amalfi · Via Fra Gerardo Sasso
✦ Michelin ★ NYT Eater Day 1
About✦ Michelin
Family-owned since 1872, specialising in hand-rolled pasta and morning-caught seafood. The scialatielli ai frutti di mare is the kitchen's signature dish.
CF
Caitie's note
Get the limone pasta. You won't regret it. Ask for the terrace table — non-negotiable.
💡 Tip: Book 3+ weeks ahead for dinner. Lunch is slightly easier to get.
💰 $$$$🕖 Dinner from 7pm📞 Reservations essential

02
Da Adolfo
📍 Laurito Beach, Positano · boat access only
Eater 👤 Sarah ★★★ Day 2
CF
Caitie's note
Go for lunch, stay for the afternoon swim. They ring a bell when the last boat leaves — easy to miss. The mozzarella grilled on lemon leaves is unlike anything I've eaten.
💡 Tip: Red-striped boat from Positano harbour. Cash only on the boat.
💰 $$$🕐 Lunch only⛵ Boat from harbour

Rossellinis ★★
📍 Palazzo Avino, Ravello
✦ Michelin ★★ CNT Day 3
CF

The splurge of the trip. Do the tasting menu — paced perfectly and the sunset terrace view is one of the most beautiful things I've seen while eating. Smart casual is enforced.

Things to Do 7 activities
Filter by day:
01
Path of the Gods (Sentiero degli Dei)
📍 Agerola → Nocelle · ~8km
Lonely Planet CNT 👤 Marcus ★★★ Day 1
AboutLonely Planet
Approximately 8 kilometres (5 miles) from Agerola to Nocelle, with sweeping panoramic views of the Mediterranean and the Amalfi coastline. Moderate difficulty, best walked west to east.
CF
Caitie's note
Best hike of the trip. Start before 9am. After Nocelle, take the 1,700 steps to Positano and reward yourself with lunch at Da Adolfo. Wear proper shoes.
⏱ 3-4 hrs🆓 Free💪 Moderate
127 travellers added this
02
Boat Tour to Li Galli Islands
📍 From Positano harbour
CNT Day 2
CF
Caitie's note
Book a private boat — split four ways it's totally reasonable and the difference is significant. Ask to stop at the grotto on the way back.
💡 Tip: Check weather the night before — boats cancel if seas are rough.
⏱ Half day💰 ~$120pp private

Villa Rufolo Gardens
📍 Piazza Duomo, Ravello
NYT 👤 Caitie ★★★ Day 3
CF
Caitie's note
The garden terrace at sunset is where Wagner composed — you'll understand why immediately. Go late afternoon, combine with a walk through Ravello's quiet streets.
⏱ 1-2 hrs💰 €7 entry🌅 Best at golden hour
